Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

Spaghetti Carbonara is a classic Italian pasta dish known for its simplicity and rich, creamy flavor. Made with al dente spaghetti, crispy pancetta or guanciale, and a silky sauce of eggs and cheese, it’s a delightful comfort food. Black pepper adds a hint of spice, complementing the savory flavors perfectly.

Perfect for a quick yet indulgent meal, Carbonara relies on high-quality ingredients and careful timing to achieve its signature creamy texture without using cream. It’s a dish that truly highlights the beauty of Italian cuisine’s minimalist approach.

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti
  • Eggs
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Pecorino Romano cheese
  • Pancetta or guanciale
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  1. Cook spaghetti in salted water; reserve some pasta water.
  2. Whisk eggs and cheese together; set aside.
  3. Cook pancetta in olive oil until crispy; add garlic.
  4. Toss spaghetti with pancetta; remove from heat.
  5. Mix in egg and cheese mixture, adding pasta water for creaminess.
  6. Season with salt and pepper; serve immediately.
