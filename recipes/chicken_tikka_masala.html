Chicken Tikka Masala

Description

Chicken Tikka Masala is a beloved Indian-inspired dish, featuring tender pieces of marinated chicken simmered in a rich, creamy tomato-based curry sauce. Infused with aromatic spices such as cumin, coriander, and garam masala, this dish strikes a perfect balance of smoky, tangy, and creamy flavors. Traditionally served with steamed basmati rice or warm naan bread, it’s a true comfort food classic.

This versatile dish can be customized by adjusting spice levels, using boneless chicken thighs or breasts, or incorporating vegetables like bell peppers. Its vibrant orange hue and irresistible aroma make it a favorite for family meals or dinner parties, offering a delicious taste of Indian cuisine with every bite.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken (boneless, cubed)
  • Yogurt
  • Lemon juice
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Garam masala
  • Cumin
  • Turmeric
  • Paprika
  • Tomato puree
  • Heavy cream
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Cilantro (for garnish)
  1. Marinate chicken in yogurt, lemon juice, ginger-garlic paste, and spices for at least 1 hour.
  2. Cook the marinated chicken in a skillet until lightly charred; set aside.
  3. Sauté onions and garlic in olive oil until soft.
  4. Add tomato puree, cream, and spices; simmer until thickened.
  5. Stir in the cooked chicken and simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. Garnish with cilantro and serve with rice or naan.
